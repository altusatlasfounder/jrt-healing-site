仁 Our Name

吉 仁 堂

Hall of Fortunate Benevolence

吉 Jí Auspicious, fortunate — the hope that healing begins the moment you walk through the door.
仁 Rén Compassion, benevolence — the heart of every treatment we provide.
堂 Táng A hall, a gathering place — the sanctuary where healing comes together.

There is also a quiet nod embedded in the tradition: Suān Zǎo Rén (酸枣仁) — sour jujube seed — a revered classical herb used to calm the spirit and restore restful sleep. A reminder that the most powerful healing is often the most ancient.
